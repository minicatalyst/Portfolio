Marilyn Monroe

The things you didn't know about this icon!

Here are 10 quick facts about Marilyn Monroe that you probably didn't know!

  • 1 - She had an arranged marriage to Joe DiMaggio at 16.
  • 2 - Her birth name was Norma Jean Baker.
  • 3 - She was in and out of foster care.
  • 4 - Her mother was in psychiatric care for bipolar disorder. And she never knew her father.
  • 5 - She would often refer to herself in the 3rd person.
  • 6 - She was scared to be in the camera and often would have a stress rash anytime she had to perform.
  • 7 - Monroe was into intellectual men and had a list of people she wanted to sleep with. One of the names on the list was "Albert Einstein".
  • 8 - There is an FBI file on her.
  • 9 - After she died, the couple that moved into her house had found out that the entire house had been bugged.
  • 10 - Marilyn monroe was a book enthusiust and loved to read. After she passed, over 400 volumes were found on her shelves.
